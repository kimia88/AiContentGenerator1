Introduction

In the midst of the pandemic, parents worldwide are grappling with the dual challenges of maintaining their professional commitments while shouldering the responsibility of homeschooling their children. This unprecedented situation has thrust many into a role they are ill-prepared for, leading to increased stress levels and anxiety. The shift to remote work has blurred the boundaries between personal and professional life, making it difficult for parents to find a balance. Moreover, the lack of social interaction and support systems exacerbates the pressure. This article delves into the struggles faced by parents, offering insights and strategies to manage this delicate equilibrium during these trying times.
