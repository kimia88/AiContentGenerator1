Introduction

Education is a transformative force that shapes individuals and societies. It fosters personal development by cultivating knowledge, critical thinking, and social skills. Schools prepare students for the workforce through vocational training and essential life skills like discipline and teamwork. Education also drives societal progress and economic growth; educated individuals have lower unemployment rates and higher earnings, contributing to a more prosperous and equitable society. According to the World Bank, each additional year of schooling can increase an individual's earnings by 10% on average. Education promotes equality and social mobility, offering opportunities for all, regardless of background. Thus, schools play a pivotal role in shaping a brighter future for individuals and communities alike.
