Introduction

Medicines play a vital role in preventing, treating, and alleviating symptoms of various health conditions, thereby improving overall well-being. They are substances intended for use in the diagnosis, treatment, or prevention of disease. Medicines can be classified into several categories based on their uses, such as antibiotics for bacterial infections, antivirals for viral infections, pain relievers for managing pain, and vaccines for preventing diseases. Proper use of medicines, following healthcare provider's advice, is crucial to ensure their effectiveness and minimize potential side effects. It's also important to understand that not all medicines are suitable for everyone, and factors like age, health status, and potential drug interactions must be considered when prescribing or taking medicines.
