Introduction

Cristiano Ronaldo is a legendary Portuguese footballer celebrated for his extraordinary skills and numerous achievements. Born in Madeira, Portugal, Ronaldo began his professional career with Sporting CP before moving to Manchester United in 2003. Under Sir Alex Ferguson, he developed into one of the world's best players, helping United win three Premier League titles and the UEFA Champions League in 2008. In 2009, he joined Real Madrid, where he spent nine seasons, winning four Champions League titles and two La Liga championships. Ronaldo's individual honors include five Ballon d'Or awards and four European Golden Shoes. In 2018, he moved to Juventus, winning the Serie A title in his first season. He returned to Manchester United in 2021. Off the pitch, Ronaldo is known for his philanthropy and endorsement deals.
