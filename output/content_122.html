Introduction

Fashion is more than just a means of protection; it's a powerful form of self-expression and identity assertion. Throughout history, clothing has been used to convey social status, cultural affiliation, and personal style. In contemporary society, fashion serves as a canvas for individuals to paint their unique narratives, reflecting their personalities, beliefs, and aspirations. From haute couture runways to street style, fashion trends continually evolve, mirroring societal changes and influencing cultural norms. Moreover, fashion plays a significant role in the global economy, driving innovation and employment across various sectors. Understanding the multifaceted nature of fashion provides insights into human creativity, commerce, and communication.
